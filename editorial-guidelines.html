Editorial Guidelines for Quoc Khanh Uniform Mobile App

These Editorial Guidelines outline the standards, principles, and processes that govern the content published and displayed in the Quoc Khanh Uniform mobile application (the "App").

Effective date: 01 December 2025
Legal entity: CÔNG TY TNHH QUỐC KHÁNH UNIFORM VIỆT NAM (QUOC KHANH UNIFORM CO.,LTD)
Website: https://uniform-quockhanh.store

1. Purpose and Scope

The Quoc Khanh Uniform mobile app is designed to provide users with information about our company, services, and news related to uniform design and manufacturing. This app serves as an informational and communication platform for customers and potential clients.

These Editorial Guidelines apply to all content published within the App, including but not limited to:

  • Company information and descriptions
  • Service offerings and product information
  • News articles and blog posts
  • Contact forms and communication content
  • Images, graphics, and visual materials

2. Content Standards

All content published in the App must adhere to the following standards:

2.1 Accuracy and Truthfulness

  • All information must be accurate, current, and truthful.
  • Company information, contact details, and service descriptions must reflect actual business operations.
  • Claims about products, services, or capabilities must be verifiable and not misleading.

2.2 Professionalism

  • Content must maintain a professional tone appropriate for a business application.
  • Language should be clear, concise, and free from offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate material.
  • Visual content (images, logos, graphics) must be of high quality and relevant to the business context.

2.3 Legal Compliance

  • All content must comply with applicable Vietnamese laws and regulations.
  • Content must not infringe upon intellectual property rights, including copyrights, trademarks, or patents.
  • Content must not contain false advertising, deceptive practices, or violate consumer protection laws.

3. Content Review Process

All content published in the App undergoes an internal review process to ensure compliance with these Editorial Guidelines:

  • Pre-publication review: All new content is reviewed by authorized personnel before being published.
  • Regular audits: Existing content is periodically reviewed and updated to ensure accuracy and relevance.
  • Correction process: Any identified errors or outdated information are corrected promptly upon discovery.

4. User-Generated Content

Currently, the App does not allow users to publish or submit content directly. If this functionality is added in the future, additional guidelines will be established to govern user-generated content, including moderation policies and user conduct standards.

5. News and Blog Content

News articles and blog posts published in the App are subject to the following guidelines:

  • Articles must be relevant to the uniform manufacturing industry, company updates, or related business topics.
  • Sources and references must be credible and properly attributed when applicable.
  • Publication dates must be accurate and clearly displayed.
  • Content must not contain unsubstantiated claims or false information.

6. Contact and Communication

The App includes contact forms and communication features. Guidelines for these features include:

  • Contact information provided must be accurate and regularly monitored.
  • Responses to user inquiries should be professional, timely, and helpful.
  • User privacy must be respected in all communications, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

7. Updates and Revisions

These Editorial Guidelines may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices, legal requirements, or app functionality. The "Effective date" at the top of this document will be revised when updates are made. We recommend reviewing these guidelines periodically.

8. Reporting Concerns

If you encounter content in the App that you believe violates these Editorial Guidelines, or if you have concerns about the accuracy or appropriateness of any content, please contact us using the information provided below.
